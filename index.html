About Us

Our primary objective is to create a website and app that will allow patients or their relatives to find and connect with in-home caregivers, and to support the well-being of patients after placement. The website will allow patients or their relatives to find caregivers in their area, bookmark them for later, and schedule interviews with caregivers to ensure they are a good fit. Additionally, caregivers will be able to schedule event reminders for their patients. They will also have a feature on the app that allows them to directly get access to EMS (emergency medical services) to receive extra support in case of possible patient emergencies. Our website will help patients and/or their relatives make informed decisions regarding their caregiver and facilitate the meeting between the parties, and will not force nor attempt to bias a patient or their relative into choosing one caregiver over another. We will build the front end of the website, with all of its functionality, as well as build a back end database that stores user information in a secure manner. Our estimated budget needed for this project is $38,248.57. We have no connection to the agency mission, business impact, or other projects.
